Staking is 14 dayS lock period for 329.92% APY (Annual Percentage Yield). This is subject to change in future.

What is the maximum tokens allowed to stake?

Maximum tokens allowed to be staked is 20,000,000 $Rocko tokens.

What if I unstaked early is there any tax or fee?
